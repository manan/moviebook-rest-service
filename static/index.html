About Moviebook

Moviebook is a fun and quirky way to share movies with your friends. Upload a picture of your favourite scene, type in your favourite dialogue and brag about your favourite movies. We're building Moviebook to paint a shade of fantasy to your life. We imagine a world more connected through movies.
